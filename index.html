Winn Chain: The High-Performance Layer-1, Built for the Future of dApps

Experience Ethereum-compatible development with unparalleled speed, low-cost transactions, and a security-first Proof-of-Stake architecture. Build, scale, and innovate without compromise

Start Building on Testnet Explore Enterprise Use Cases

Engineered for Performance and Scale

  • Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) Compatibility

    Built with full EVM compatibility, Winn Chain allows you to deploy your existing Solidity smart contracts and dApps with minimal friction. Leverage familiar tools like Remix, Hardhat, and Truffle while enjoying superior throughput and lower costs

  • Secure, Sustainable, and Energy-Efficient

    Our robust Proof-of-Stake (PoS) mechanism secures the network, eliminates the massive energy footprint of Proof-of-Work, and opens revenue streams for validators and delegators. Participate in network governance and earn rewards for securing the chain

  • High Throughput, Instant Finality

    Winn Chain's optimized architecture is engineered for high-speed transactions and instant finality, supporting thousands of transactions per second. Whether you're building a high-frequency DEX or a mainstream NFT game, our network won't be your bottleneck
